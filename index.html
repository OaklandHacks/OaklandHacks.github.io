OHACKS II


The​ ​first​ ​high​ ​school​ ​hackathon​ ​in​ ​Oakland​ ​run​ ​by​ ​high​ ​school​ ​students.​ ​A Hackathon​ ​is​ ​an​ ​event​ in​ ​which​ ​a​ ​large​ ​number​ ​of​ ​people​ ​meet​ ​to​ ​engage​ ​in​ ​collaborative computer​ ​programming.​ ​During​ ​OHacks​ ​II,​ Participants​ ​will​ ​build​ ​and​ ​present​ ​their​ ​creations​ ​in​ ​just 12​ ​hours! OHacks II will be held at the Kapor Center (Oakland, CA) on April 22, 2017.

Where

Kapor Center - Oakland, CA

Oakland Hacks II will be hosted  on April 22nd at the Kapor Center in Downtown Oakland. It is walking distance from the 19th street BART station. The address is  2148 Broadway, Oakland, CA 94612.

Who

High Schoolers - SF Bay Area

OHacks II is targeted toward Oakland High Schoolers. However it is open to all high school students that would like to participate. If you’ve already graduated from high school, you can’t attend as a participant, but you can come help mentor others.

MORE

Why

Raise Awareness - Interest, Diversity

We are hosting this hackathon in oakland because we aim to improve diversity in the tech world. Students of color and girls are historically underrepresented  and we would like to provide support to the introduction and continued education of minorities in tech.
