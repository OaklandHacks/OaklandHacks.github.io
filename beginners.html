HACKATHONS 101: 

Well to start, a hackathon is an event in which a large number of people meet to engage in collaborative computer programming During OHacks II, participants will build and present their creations in just 12 hours!.

WHAT TO DO AT OHACKS II:

If you are brand new to coding and would still like to attend OHacks II, we recommend looking at websites like codecademy.com or codeschool.com to get started. And even if you don’t completely learn everything, there will be plenty of mentors that will be available to help you and your team along the way!
